Understanding Lightspeed WooCommerce Integration

Lightspeed WooCommerce integration is the process of synchronizing data between your Lightspeed POS system and WooCommerce online store. Once integrated, both platforms share real-time information, ensuring that inventory levels, product details, and orders remain accurate across channels.
